About

Preston Matchett is one of Arizona's most trusted real estate agents. As a member of eXp Realty, Preston works with individuals, investors and developers who are interested in some of the most coveted properties throughout Arizona. As a former professional athlete, Preston has an extremely strong work ethic and is committed to work tirelessly for his clients. A high-touch agent with extensive market and economic knowledge and unmatched devotion to his clients, you will feel confident knowing that he will offer his candid advice and fight for you. After graduating from Seattle University in 2013 with a marketing and finance degree, Preston moved to Arizona full-time to train while playing professional golf around the world. Preston works closely with numerous local organizations and charities including the Wounded Warrior Project which supports veterans and service members who incurred a physical or mental injury, illness, or wound while serving in the military. When he isn't working, Preston enjoys fishing, traveling, helping out on his parents' ranch in Montana, and spending time with family and friends.
